Rabbi Avrohom Dov Kahn has been teaching and inspiring Jews of all backgrounds for four decades. His love and caring for all Jews is well known. He studied under Rabbi Joseph B. Soloveichik for 6 Â½ years and received his Rabbinic Ordination (Yoreh Yoreh, Yodin Yodin) from Rabbi Moshe Feinstein in 1972. He has a B.A. from CCNY and a M.B.A from NYU. In 1981, he founded the Center for Return in order to bring Jewish college students and young professionals closer to Judaism. Rabbi Kahn and Center for Return are responsible for over 500 Jews becoming completely Torah observant, including our President and several of our board members. He is the author of numerous scholarly and popular articles on Torah topics and has published his own original commentary on the Passover Haggadah. Rabbi Kahn married his life partner Miriam in 1974 and they are the proud parents of six and grandparents of 20 and counting.
