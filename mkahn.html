Rebetzin Kahn, a native of Detroit, Michigan studied at the Michlala in Israel for two years and graduated from Queens College. She is an accomplished Judaica silversmith and this artistic creativity is evident in the CFR literature and programming. Over the years, Mrs. Kahn has hosted thousands for Shabbat, holidays, and events where her culinary expertise is very much in evidence. Her warmth and friendliness are responsible for many hundreds of Jews returning to their heritage.
